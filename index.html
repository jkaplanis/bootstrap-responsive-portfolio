About Me

After graduating from UC Santa Cruz in 2011, I began a career in hospitality with a focus on front office management. After spending a few years working in hotels I decided it was time for a change. In the years that followed I had the opportunity to work for a larger emerging growth company as well as a smaller startup.

I am currently enrolled in the coding bootcamp at UCSD where I am developing the skills needed to become a full-stack web developer. I look forward to combining this new skillset with my previous experience in order to launch my new career.
